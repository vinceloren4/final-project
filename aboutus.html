Our Journey

It all started on a quiet afternoon with our final HTML project looming over us. At first, everything seemed chaotic tags didn’t close, divs went missing, and our design ideas clashed. But we quickly realized that working together, we could turn mistakes into learning moments.

We divided tasks strategically: Gabriel focused on design and styling, while Vince handled the structure and coding logic. Sometimes we swapped roles, learning from each other and laughing at our missteps. Every line of code strengthened our collaboration and creativity.

By the night before the deadline, tired but determined, we watched our project come alive on the browser. It wasn’t just a website—it was a reflection of our teamwork, patience, and friendship. Our journey taught us that creating something together is far more meaningful than the final result itself.
